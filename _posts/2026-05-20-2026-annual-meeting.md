We are excited to announce that the **2026 OHBM Australia Meeting** is scheduled for **Friday October 23, 2026**. The meeting will be hosted in QIMR Berghofer, Brisbane. Please lock in the date in your calendars!

We look forward to welcoming you to Brisbane.

Our goal is to unite the Australian human brain mapping community.
